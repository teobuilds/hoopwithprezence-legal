Privacy Policy

Hoop With Prezence • Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hoop With Prezence ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your email address and name when you create an account or sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Profile information — optional details you provide such as position, height, graduation year, dominant hand, school, and profile photo.
  • Content you upload — basketball training videos and related metadata you choose to upload for analysis.
  • AI analysis data — feedback and recommendations generated from your content and training activity.
  • Usage and diagnostic data — app activity, performance, and crash reports used to keep the app stable.
  • Subscription status — whether you hold an active subscription (purchases are processed by Apple; we do not receive your payment card details).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core features: training programs, video uploads, and AI-powered analysis.
  • To create and manage your account and authenticate sign-in.
  • To process and restore subscriptions.
  • To improve app performance and diagnose crashes.
  • To communicate important service updates and reminders.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers that process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and storage of your account data and uploaded content.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and entitlement verification.
  • Apple & Google Sign-In — optional authentication.
  • Google (Gemini AI) — processing of content to generate training analysis.
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting (no payment data).

Each provider processes data under its own privacy terms. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from within the app, which permanently removes your profile, uploaded content, and associated data from our systems.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or update your profile information in the app.
  • Delete your account and associated data in-app at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data or ask questions by contacting us (below).

6. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@hoopwithprezence.com.
